radio单选/checkbox多选按钮

单选示例及代码

用法:只需在input[type='radio']元素中添加类样式即可，默认蓝色样式，改变样式，只需加上相应的类名即可

单选radio无动画效果

名称 组合
默认单选按钮 class="bdtd-radios-label"
绿色单选按钮 class="bdtd-radios-label radio-green-style"
显示代码 
      
          <label class="bdtd-radios-label">
              <input type="radio" name="sex"><i class="bdtd-radios"></i><span class="bdtd-radio-span">男</span>
          </label>
          <label class="bdtd-radios-label">
              <input type="radio" name="sex"><i class="bdtd-radios"></i><span class="bdtd-radio-span">女</span>
          </label>
          <label class="bdtd-radios-label radio-green-style">
              <input type="radio" name="sex"><i class="bdtd-radios"></i><span class="bdtd-radio-span">蔬菜</span>
          </label>
          <label class="bdtd-radios-label radio-green-style">
              <input type="radio" name="sex"><i class="bdtd-radios"></i><span class="bdtd-radio-span">水果</span>
          </label>
